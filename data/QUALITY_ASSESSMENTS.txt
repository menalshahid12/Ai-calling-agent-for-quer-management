IST QUALITY ENHANCEMENT AND ASSESSMENTS (2011–2025)

IST has a Quality Enhancement Cell (QEC) that ensures academic standards and continuous improvement. All programs undergo regular Self Assessment Reports (SAR) reviewed by assessment teams. IST follows HEC quality assurance guidelines.

Accreditation: All engineering programs are accredited by PEC (Pakistan Engineering Council) under the Washington Accord Level II. Computing programs are accredited by NCEAC. All programs are HEC approved.

QEC is responsible for quality assurance, self assessment, feedback systems, and institutional quality culture. The Director QEC is a member of statutory bodies of IST including the Academic Council, Board of Studies, Board of Advanced Studies, and Curriculum Review Committee.

=== QEC TRAINING AND WORKSHOPS (selected) ===
The QEC has conducted over 75 training sessions, workshops, and awareness seminars from 2009 to 2024 covering:
- Internal Quality Audit
- Self Assessment Report (SAR) preparation and assessment
- Outcome Based Education (OBE)
- Bloom's Taxonomy in course design
- Teaching strategies and competency
- ISO 9001 awareness
- Complex Engineering Problems

When asked about quality assessments, quality assurance, QEC, accreditation, or self assessment at IST: answer from the data above. IST has been conducting quality assessments every year since 2011 across all departments.
