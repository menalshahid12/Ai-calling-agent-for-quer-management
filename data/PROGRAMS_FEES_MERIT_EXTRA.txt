IST PROGRAMS, FEES, AND CLOSING MERIT – Computer Engineering, Software Engineering, Biotechnology

PROGRAMS OFFERED (BS):
IST offers the following undergraduate programs: Aerospace Engineering, Avionics Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Materials Science and Engineering, Computer Science, Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Space Science, Mathematics, Physics, Biotechnology. Computer Engineering is offered under the Department of Electrical Engineering. Software Engineering, Computer Science, AI, and Data Science are under the Department of Computing.

FEES (per semester, BS):
- Department of Computing: BS Computer Science, BS Software Engineering, BS Artificial Intelligence, BS Data Science – all about 1 lakh 26 thousand rupees per semester.
- BS Computer Engineering (under Department of Electrical Engineering): About 1 lakh 48 thousand rupees per semester (same as Electrical Engineering).

CLOSING MERIT (last 2 years – 2023 and 2024):
- BS Computer Engineering: 2023 closing aggregate 76.8, 2024 closing aggregate 77.0.
- BS Software Engineering: 2023 closing aggregate 76.5, 2024 closing aggregate 76.8.
- BS Biotechnology: This program is offered for the first time this year. There is no previous closing merit data. Say: "Biotechnology is offered for the first time this year, so there is no previous closing merit to share."
